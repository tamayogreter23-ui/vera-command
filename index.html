Live gain checker & letting winners run

When a trade is running past your target, this shows exactly where you stand right now — then the rules for capturing the extra instead of capping at 10%.

RULE 1 — TAKE PARTIALS
At your target (say +10%), sell part of it (e.g. half) to lock profit. The rest is now playing with house money.
RULE 2 — TRAIL THE STOP
Move your stop up to break-even, then keep trailing it under the move. Let the runner run — this is where the +40% days come from.
RULE 3 — PLAN THE EXIT, DON'T GET GREEDY
Have a plan for the runner too (a level, or "trail until it stops me out"). Giving back a big gain hurts more than taking a small one.
